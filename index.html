About Me

Hello, I’m Jake from Eagle, ID! I’m currently pursuing a degree in Cybersecurity with a minor in Japanese Studies and Military Science at Boise State University. I have experience in Python, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, and I’m passionate about applying these skills in both cybersecurity and software development.

I have also served in the Idaho Army National Guard under multiple roles.

I have experience as a cyber defense intern through Boise State University's Cyberdome project.

My career interests include Information & Cyber Security, Full Stack Development, and Web and Software Architecture. While cybersecurity is my primary passion, I’m equally excited about opportunities in software engineering and development.

I also enjoy exploring the intersection of technology, culture, and language—and when I’m not working on tech projects, you’ll probably find me outdoors or learning more about the UAS (Unmanned Aerial Systems) industry.
